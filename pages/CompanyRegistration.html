Company Registration

In India, company registration is also known as incorporation of a company or formation of a business entity. Registering your company helps you to make your business as a distinguished entity and gives it a legal existence also.

The process of company registration in India is done under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs(MCA).There are different Registrar of Companies in every state to regulate the working or compliances of such companies. ROC Ahmedabad is located in the middle of the city near 22 Godown Circle.

Consult Now

The incorporation of Companies in India or any foreign companies is regulated by the Companies Act,2013 and gets its approval from CRC, Manesar.

Different Types of Company Registration in Ahmedabad

  • Private Limited Company
  • Nidhi Company
  • One Person Company
  • Section 8 Company
  • Public Limited Company
  • Limited Liability Partnership

Fees for Company Registration in Ahmedabad

The govt. fees for registration of a company depends on the capital investment and the number of members involved in incorporation of the company. Taking an example of capital of Rs. 1 lakh and 2 members and place of Business is in Rajasthan; it costs around Rs. 1500/-

List of the Documents Required for Company Registration in Ahmedabad

  • PAN CARD
  • AADHAAR CARD
  • LATEST BANK STATEMENT
  • RENT AGREEMENT AND ELECTRICITY BILL

Process to Register Company in Ahmedabad

The whole process of Company registration can be divided into four steps:

  • 1

    Application for Digital Signature Certificate(DSC)

  • 2

    Application for the name availability

  • 3

    File Form Spice+ along with other forms such as E-MOA, E-AOA, AGILE.

  • 4

    Issued certificate of incorporation by ROC with PAN and TAN

  • 5

    Opening a current bank account on the company name and deposit the amount of capital subscribed

  • Trading of Goods of any kind and many more

Conclusion

Registration of Company in Ahmedabad is beneficial as it becomes Legal Entity, has Perpetual Succession, Limited Liability, Can Sue or can Be Sued, and has its own Borrowing Capacity. Keeping in mind the benefits of company registration it is advisable to go with the company registration process.
